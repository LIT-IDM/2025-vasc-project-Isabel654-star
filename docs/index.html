This is an interactive Augmented Reality (AR) mobile application built in Unity, designed to bring a fun, festive experience into your world.

Instructions

This app is only available for Android.

  1. Tap the Download APK button.
  2. Install the app on your Android device.
  3. Once installed, tap the Open button to begin the experience.

How to enjoy the experience

  1. Open the app after installation.
  2. Point your camera at the Present Marker located on the physical Christmas tree.
  3. Interact with the AR elements.
